# SRE Knowledge Flow

## Overview

In SRE environments, knowledge is often generated during incidents, operational changes, reviews, and postmortems.

## Proposed flow

1. An incident or need is identified.
2. The work is registered and documented.
3. A change is proposed through a pull request.
4. Validation checks are executed.
5. Approved knowledge is published.
6. The content is reused in future incidents and reviews.

## Alignment with SRE

This flow supports:

- observability-informed response
- operational consistency
- learning from incidents
- reduced toil through automation
